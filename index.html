Hervey's Story

I was born in Orlando, and raised between Atlanta and El Salvador. I’m bilingual in English and Spanish and I’m half American and Honduran. I started studying marketing in El Salvador and moved to New York to finish with a Bachelors in Communications at City College of New York. I have experience in radio, customer service and government. I became interested in tech after college, and I’m interested in web development in particular because of its growing demand. I want to learn advanced skills to become a front end developer and get mentoring along the way to start my tech career.
